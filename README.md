# 🌱 AgroVital - Smart Agriculture Assistant  

Welcome to **AgroVital**! 🌾🚜 AgroVital is an intelligent agriculture assistant designed to help tea farmers maximize productivity, prevent diseases, and make data-driven decisions. With real-time IoT-based soil monitoring, AI-powered disease recognition, and personalized recommendations, AgroVital ensures your crops receive the best care possible.  

Let’s revolutionize tea farming together! 🍃  



## 📋 Features  

### 🌿 Tea Disease Recognition 🦠  
- AI-powered image recognition to detect common tea plant diseases.  
- Get instant identification with actionable treatment recommendations.  
- Prevent crop loss by taking early corrective measures.  

### 🌍 Real-time Soil Monitoring (IoT) ⏳  
- Uses **NPK sensors** to measure **Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium** levels.  
- Provides **real-time soil health insights** for better nutrient management.  
- Alerts when nutrient levels are unbalanced to prevent soil degradation.  

### ☁️ Weather Updates & Smart Recommendations ⛅  
- Live weather forecasts tailored to your farm location.  
- Smart insights on **when to water, fertilize, or protect crops** from extreme weather.  
- Notifications for **rain, drought, and temperature changes** affecting crop yield.  

### 📌 Personalized Recommendations 📊  
- AI-driven **crop management suggestions** based on disease reports, soil health, and weather conditions.  
- **Fertilizer & pesticide usage optimization** to enhance sustainability.  
- Custom alerts for plant care and harvesting schedules.  

### 📚 Disease Library 📖  
- Extensive database of tea plant diseases with **detailed symptoms, causes, and treatments**.  
- High-quality images to help farmers **identify issues quickly**.  
- Regular updates with the **latest agricultural research**.  

### 💬 Community Forum 👥  
- Connect with other farmers, agronomists, and experts.  
- Share experiences, ask questions, and get real-time advice.  
- Stay updated on **best practices and innovations in tea farming**.  

### 🤖 AgroBot - Your Smart Farming Assistant 💡  
- Chatbot-powered **virtual assistant** for instant help.  
- Ask farming-related questions and get **quick, AI-driven responses**.  
- Available **24/7** for guidance on crops, soil health, and weather.  

### 🛒 Marketplace 
- A digital hub to **connect farmers, suppliers, and agricultural stores**.  
- Buy and sell **fertilizers, seeds, pesticides, farming equipment, and more**.  
- Direct messaging feature to **negotiate prices and place orders**.
